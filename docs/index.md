# Open Clinical Terminology (`oct`)

Welcome to the documentation for Open Clinical Terminology (`oct`).

This site is organised for three main audiences:

- **Contributors** – people who want to help build and govern `oct`.
- **Informaticians** – people interested in the clinical/semantic model, use in systems, and integration with other standards.
- **Developers** – people building tools, integrations, and applications that use `oct`.

Use the navigation on the left to explore guidance and reference material for your role.
